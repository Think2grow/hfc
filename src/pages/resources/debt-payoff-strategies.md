Paying off debt can feel overwhelming, but with the right strategy, you can make real progress. Here are some of the best ways to tackle debt.

## Step 1: List Your Debts
Write down each debt, balance, interest rate, and minimum payment.

## Step 2: Choose a Payoff Method
- **Debt Snowball:** Pay off the smallest balance first for quick wins.
- **Debt Avalanche:** Pay off the highest interest rate first to save money.

## Step 3: Make a Plan
- Pay minimums on all debts
- Put extra money toward your chosen target debt
- Celebrate each payoff!

## Tips for Success
- Avoid taking on new debt
- Look for ways to increase income (side gigs, selling items)
- Ask for lower interest rates or payment plans
