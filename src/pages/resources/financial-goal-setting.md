## Why Set Financial Goals?
- Gives you direction and motivation
- Helps you measure progress
- Turns dreams into action steps

## How to Set Goals
1. Be specific ("Save $1,000 for vacation by June")
2. Make them measurable and time-bound
3. Break big goals into small steps
4. Write them down and review often

## Tracking Progress
- Use a spreadsheet, app, or notebook
- Celebrate milestones
- Adjust as life changes

## Common Goals
- Build an emergency fund
- Pay off debt
- Save for a home, car, or trip
- Invest for retirement
